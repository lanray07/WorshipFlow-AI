# WorshipFlow AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

WorshipFlow AI is an organizational worship planning app for churches and worship teams.

## Data Collection

WorshipFlow AI is designed to work locally on your device with mock AI enabled by default. The app does not collect personal data from users for tracking or advertising.

Information you enter in the app, such as church profile details, songs, set lists, rehearsal notes, volunteer details, and scheduling information, is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export it.

## AI Features

WorshipFlow AI includes mock AI planning suggestions by default. Any future remote AI functionality should be routed through a secure backend. API keys should never be stored in the app.

AI suggestions are optional organizational recommendations only. Users remain responsible for ministry decisions.

## Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple through the App Store. WorshipFlow AI does not receive or store your App Store payment details.

## Notifications

If enabled, WorshipFlow AI may send local reminders for rehearsals, services, or volunteer scheduling. These notifications are generated on your device.

## Data Sharing

WorshipFlow AI does not sell personal data. The app does not share user data with third-party advertisers.

## Data Deletion

You can delete app data from within the app settings where available, or by deleting the app from your device.
