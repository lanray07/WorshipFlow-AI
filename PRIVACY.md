# WorshipFlow AI Privacy Policy

WorshipFlow AI is designed as an offline-friendly worship planning and organization app.

The app does not collect personal data for tracking or advertising. Worship planning data such as church profile details, songs, set lists, volunteer information, rehearsal notes, and service timeline content is stored locally on the device unless a future sync feature is explicitly enabled by the user.

Mock AI features run as planning suggestions inside the app by default. Users remain responsible for ministry decisions, scheduling, worship content, and service planning.

The app may use Apple frameworks such as StoreKit for subscriptions, local notifications for reminders, speech recognition for voice input, and native sharing/export features when requested by the user. Those Apple services are governed by Apple's privacy terms and platform controls.

For support or privacy questions, open an issue at:
https://github.com/lanray07/WorshipFlow-AI/issues
